Colors

_heart.scss

Colors are kept light and playful, but neutral. Colors are provided as contextual accessories and supporting colors, not as brand colors.

Any site should override whatever colors they need to match their brand.

Color Usage

All colors have supporting classes to easily add color to elements and components:

  • _color-red
  • _color-bg-red
  • _color-border-red —requires border styling
